union type for wrapped events in stream PROPOSAL is mapped to governance data, something to keep in mind

```graphql
union Event = TimeUpdate | MarketEvent | TransferResponses | PositionResolution | Order | Trade | Account | Party | MarginLevels | Proposal | Vote | MarketData | NodeSignature | LossSocialization | SettlePosition | Market | Asset | MarketTick | SettleDistressed | AuctionEvent | RiskFactor | Deposit | Withdrawal | OracleSpec | LiquidityProvision
```


### Possible types

#### [`TimeUpdate`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/time-update)




#### [`MarketEvent`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/market-event)




#### [`TransferResponses`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/transfer-responses)




#### [`PositionResolution`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/position-resolution)




#### [`Order`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/order)

An order in Vega, if active it will be on the OrderBook for the market


#### [`Trade`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/trade)

A trade on Vega, the result of two orders being 'matched' in the market


#### [`Account`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/account)

An account record


#### [`Party`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/party)

Represents a party on Vega, could be an ethereum wallet address in the future


#### [`MarginLevels`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/margin-levels)

Margins for a given a party


#### [`Proposal`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/proposal)




#### [`Vote`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/vote)




#### [`MarketData`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/market-data)

Live data of a Market


#### [`NodeSignature`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/node-signature)

Represents a signature for the approval of a resource from a validator


#### [`LossSocialization`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/loss-socialization)




#### [`SettlePosition`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/settle-position)




#### [`Market`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/market)

Represents a product & associated parameters that can be traded on Vega, has an associated OrderBook and Trade history


#### [`Asset`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/asset)

Represents an asset in vega


#### [`MarketTick`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/market-tick)




#### [`SettleDistressed`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/settle-distressed)




#### [`AuctionEvent`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/auction-event)




#### [`RiskFactor`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/risk-factor)

A risk factor emitted by the risk model for a given market


#### [`Deposit`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/deposit)

The details of a deposit processed by vega


#### [`Withdrawal`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/withdrawal)

The details of a withdrawal processed by vega


#### [`OracleSpec`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/oracle-spec)

An oracle spec describe the oracle data that a product (or a risk model)
wants to get from the oracle engine.


#### [`LiquidityProvision`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/liquidity-provision)

The command to be sent to the chain for a liquidity provision submission
