Candle stick representation of trading

```graphql
type Candle {
  timestamp: String!
  datetime: String!
  high: String!
  low: String!
  open: String!
  close: String!
  volume: String!
  interval: Interval!
}
```


### Fields

#### `timestamp` ([`String`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/string))

Unix epoch+nanoseconds for when the candle occurred


#### `datetime` ([`String`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/string))

RFC3339Nano formatted date and time for the candle


#### `high` ([`String`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/string))

High price (uint64)


#### `low` ([`String`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/string))

Low price (uint64)


#### `open` ([`String`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/string))

Open price (uint64)


#### `close` ([`String`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/string))

Close price (uint64)


#### `volume` ([`String`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/string))

Volume price (uint64)


#### `interval` ([`Interval`](/docs/testnet/graphql/enums/interval))

Interval price (string)
