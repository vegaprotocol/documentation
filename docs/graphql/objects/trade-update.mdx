A trade on Vega, the result of two orders being 'matched' in the market

```graphql
type TradeUpdate {
  id: ID!
  marketId: ID!
  buyOrder: String!
  sellOrder: String!
  buyerId: ID!
  sellerId: ID!
  aggressor: Side!
  price: String!
  size: String!
  createdAt: String!
  type: TradeType!
  buyerFee: TradeFee!
  sellerFee: TradeFee!
  buyerAuctionBatch: Int
  sellerAuctionBatch: Int
}
```


### Fields

#### [`id`](#) ([`ID!`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/id))

The hash of the trade data


#### [`marketId`](#) ([`ID!`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/id))

The market the trade occurred on


#### [`buyOrder`](#) ([`String!`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/string))

The order that bought


#### [`sellOrder`](#) ([`String!`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/string))

The order that sold


#### [`buyerId`](#) ([`ID!`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/id))

The party that bought


#### [`sellerId`](#) ([`ID!`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/id))

The party that sold


#### [`aggressor`](#) ([`Side!`](/docs/testnet/graphql/enums/side))

The aggressor indicates whether this trade was related to a BUY or SELL


#### [`price`](#) ([`String!`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/string))

The price of the trade (probably initially the passive order price, other determination algorithms are possible though) (uint64)


#### [`size`](#) ([`String!`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/string))

The number of units traded, will always be &#x003C;= the remaining size of both orders immediately before the trade (uint64)


#### [`createdAt`](#) ([`String!`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/string))

RFC3339Nano time for when the trade occurred


#### [`type`](#) ([`TradeType!`](/docs/testnet/graphql/enums/trade-type))

The type of trade


#### [`buyerFee`](#) ([`TradeFee!`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/trade-fee))

The fee paid by the buyer side of the trade


#### [`sellerFee`](#) ([`TradeFee!`](/docs/testnet/graphql/objects/trade-fee))

The fee paid by the seller side of the trade


#### [`buyerAuctionBatch`](#) ([`Int`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/int))

The batch in which the buyer order was submitted (applies only for auction modes)


#### [`sellerAuctionBatch`](#) ([`Int`](/docs/testnet/graphql/scalars/int))

The batch in which the seller order was submitted (applies only for auction modes)
